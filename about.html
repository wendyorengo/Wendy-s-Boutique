About us

The boutique was established in 2022, in Kampala Uganda. It is located at Friend's plaza along Buganda Avenue. It mainly deals with fashionable, designable and affordable clothes, shoes and accesories. Our esteemed customers are treated with respect and concern as we meet their expectations. We also offer free deliveries to your orders. To place an order all is needed is to contact us through our contact number and social media pages.
